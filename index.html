From VOD Bangla

VOD Bangla offers regularly updated articles on its news website. The broadcaster's stated goals are to produce reliable news coverage, provide access to the Bangla language, and promote understanding between peoples. It is also a provider of live streaming world news which can be viewed via its website, YouTube, and various mobile devices and digital media players. VOD Bangla has been broadcasting since 2024.
